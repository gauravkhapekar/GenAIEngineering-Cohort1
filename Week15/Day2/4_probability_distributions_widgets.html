üé≤ When to Use Discrete

  • Uniform: Equal chances (dice, lottery)
  • Binomial: Count successes in trials
  • Poisson: Count events in time/space
  • Geometric: Trials until success

üìà When to Use Continuous

  • Uniform: Equal density (arrival times)
  • Normal: Natural phenomena, measurements
  • Exponential: Waiting times, lifetimes
  • Gamma: Sum of exponentials
  • Beta: Proportions, probabilities
  • Weibull: Reliability, survival analysis
