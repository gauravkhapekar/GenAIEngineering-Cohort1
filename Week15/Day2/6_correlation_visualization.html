Correlation measures the strength and direction of the relationship between two variables. It ranges from -1 to +1, where +1 indicates perfect positive correlation, -1 indicates perfect negative correlation, and 0 indicates no correlation. Correlation can be linear (following a straight line pattern) or nonlinear (following a curved or complex pattern).
